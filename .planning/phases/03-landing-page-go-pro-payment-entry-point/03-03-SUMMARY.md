  - id: BUG-1
    description: "<em>and</em> in the hero paragraph and <em>why</em> in an AI-Scan bullet visually ran together with/split away from adjacent words"
    root_cause: "frontend/index.html's Google Fonts <link> requested Plus Jakarta Sans with only Roman weights (no `ital` axis), so the browser faux-italicized <em> content by skewing the upright glyphs — the skew visually crowded the following space. Confirmed via document.fonts: no `style: 'italic'` face existed pre-fix."
    fix: "Added the `ital` axis to both the Inter and Plus Jakarta Sans Google Fonts URLs in index.html (ital,wght@0,...;1,400)."
  - id: BUG-2
    description: "\"Explains why , with the corrected step\" rendered with a large uneven gap on both sides of \"why\"; the Pro pricing card's \"Unlimited AI Scans\"/\"Unlimited AI Tutor\" lines had the same latent bug"
    root_cause: "Those <li>s use display:flex + gap:11px to space a checkmark <span> from the following text, but the <em>/<b> tag sat as a direct child of the flex container alongside plain text siblings. Flexbox blockifies every in-flow child (inline or not), so each tag became its own flex item and inherited the 11px gap on both sides. Verified empirically: setting li.style.gap = '0px' collapsed the anomalous gap to exactly 0."
    fix: "Wrapped each <li>'s text portion (excluding the checkmark span) in a single <span>, so the emphasis tag nests inside normal inline flow instead of sitting as a flex sibling."

- **Removed** the ASRJC/DHS/HCI/ACJC/CJC pill badges under "Sourced from real 2025 JC Prelim & A-Level papers" (line kept).
